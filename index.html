Welcome!

My name is Guruprasad Raghavan. I am heading to pursue my PhD at Caltech with my research interests spanning building physical/mathematical models to explain biological phenomena predominantly at the single cell scale.

Prior to this, I received my B.Tech from IIT Madras followed by a Thesis-based Masters from Carnegie Mellon University (advisor: Dr. Ge Yang).

My academic interests range from building theranostic devices to studying biological phenomena at multiple scales, with the sole aim of making a high impact contribution to the health-care system. I spend most of my spare time writing about events that excite me on my blog "Journey" and training for the IronMan 70.3 triathlon.
